The Mastermind Experience

A structured journey from intention to execution, built on the NetWorkWise methodology.

Phase 1: Foundation Workshops

This kick-off session is critical to: 1) get to know your fellow Mastermind cohort, and 2) prepare to make the most out of your Mastermind experience. The first priority of this workshop is to quickly develop relationships between and among participants using the NetWorkWise Way. Second, Adam Connors will lead an interactive session on how and why the NetWorkWise Way works, and how its values and principles will be used for Mastermind success. Lastly, the group will begin workshopping their personal goals and positioning statements.

This critical session dives deep into the importance of developing a help-first approach to networking. Participants will practice what they learn with each other, and plan for ways to be a 'giver' to someone in their own network. Participants will continue to develop personal goals and positioning statements.

This session is focused on preparing attendees to get the most out of the upcoming Executive Unlock Seminars by helping each other refine and enhance their personal goals and positioning statements using the NetWorkWise "When Statement" approach. This will help set up each participant for success at The Executive Unlock Seminars.
Phase 2: Execution & Accountability

As Adam says, "fortune is in the follow-through" and the Accountability Labs are 100% about the follow-through. This lab serves as the kick-off for turning plans into action, sharing final personal roadmaps and "when" statements, and strategies for helping each other achieve their roadmaps.

These sessions are where the magic really happens and results are realized. Each participant reviews mutual efforts, what's working, what's not and celebrate successes, with expert tutelage, advice and assistance from Adam Connors.
